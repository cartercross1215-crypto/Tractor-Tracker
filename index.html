Privacy, Terms, and Data

Privacy Policy

Tractor Tracker stores account email addresses, farm or business names, equipment records, operators, fields or job sites, jobs, maintenance reminders, completed maintenance history, customers, settings, and sync status.

Records are saved locally in this browser immediately. When you log in, the same records can also be saved online to your Tractor Tracker cloud account for syncing across devices.

Support contact: tractortracker.support@gmail.com.

Terms of Service

Tractor Tracker is a Free Unlimited Beta. Use it for testing and recordkeeping, but keep your own backups of important business records while the beta is being improved.

Do not rely on the beta as the only permanent copy of customer, billing, tax, or compliance records.

Data Deletion

Use Download All Data before deleting anything. Delete Cloud Data clears synced cloud records but keeps the account. Delete Account removes the account, cloud records, reset links, and sessions.

Local browser data can be cleared from your browser settings, or by deleting site data for Tractor Tracker.
